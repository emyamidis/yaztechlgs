Integrated Building System (IBS) Steel Frame

Yaztech engineering pioneered and specialises in design, manufacture, installation and construction of light gauge galvanized steel framing solutions mainly for residential, commercial and temporary structure throughout Southeast Asia. Our focus is to help dreamers achieve their dream construction project through holistic approach in cost and time reduction whilst ensuring long term quality.

Made to measure to Installation

Manufacturing machine imported from New Zealand, our full suite service starts off with an architectural design transferred over to a customized software for manufacturing of the steel frame to the exact specifications required. Building frame manufactured would then be assembled and transported to construction site for erection. Construction is completed with exterior and interior walls, flooring and ceiling to envelop the building proving to be the most advanced end to end steel frame building solutions.
